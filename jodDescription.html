Sales & Marketing Assistant

Job Description

We are looking for a competent and experienced Sales & Marketing Assistant and provide creative ideas to help achieve our goals. You will have administrative duties in developing and implementing sales & marketing strategies.

Job Resposibilities

  • Collect quantitative and qualitative data from sales & marketing campaigns.
  • Support the marketing team in daily administrative tasks.
  • Research and evaluate competitor marketing and digital content.
  • Assist in sales & marketing, advertising promotional activities (e.g. social media, direct mail, and web).
  • Prepare detailed promotional presentations.
  • Help distribute sales & marketing materials.
  • Manage and update company database and customer relationship management systems (CRM).
  • Help organize sales & marketing events.
  • Generate leads and build excellent rapport with corporate clients.

Requirement and Qualifications

  • Strong desire to learn along with professional drive.
  • Solid understanding of different sales & marketing techniques.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Excellent knowledge of MS Office.
  • Familiarity with marketing computer software and online applications (e.g. CRM tools, Online analytics and Google Adwords).
  • Passion for the sales & marketing industry and its best practices.
  • Work experience in Sales and Marketing.
  • Bachelor’s degree or currently working towards a bachelor’s degree in marketing, business, or a related field.

Benefits

  • 5 working days
  • Insurance Plan
  • Citizen Investment Trust (CIT)
  • Lunch facilities
  • Attractive salary for deserving candidates
  • Periodic Salary review and Grading
  • Periodic refreshment programs
